Texas Visual Arts Collective

Texas Visual Arts Collective is a creative and philanthropic student organization I co-created at UT Austin. The organization was inspired by 1) the need for a creative community catering to analog art forms and non-fine arts majors, and 2) the active spirit surrounding social issues on and off campus. While I was co-director, we:

  • Hosted two art shows selling prints and original artworks to benefit local philanthropies
  • Started a creative resale supply initiative, providing gently used creative supplies to students
  • Collaborated with community organizers to raise awareness of social justice issues like homelessness, food insecurity, and reproductive rights

Audiophile

Audiophile is a web app I made with Flask to demo an idea I had for a platform to explore audio media.

Users can find a comprehensive list of shows associated with a single podcast network, a functionality that isn’t offered on leading audio streaming platforms. Data is pulled from the iTunes Search API. Results and pagination are dynamically updated using Ajax while the rest of the page is kept static for optimal viewing. Limitations include the 200 result cap of the API.

Eventually, it will include filtering capabilities (filter podcasts by average rating, number of episodes, explicitness, etc.), music data, and a profile feature to save, organize, and review works.

This is my final project for the Harvard CS50 course, Harvard’s introductory computer science course offered online.
