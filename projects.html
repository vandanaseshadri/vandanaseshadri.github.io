Texas Visual Arts Collective

Texas Visual Arts Collective is a creative and philanthropic student organization I co-created at UT Austin. The organization was born out of 1) the need for a creative community catering to analog art forms and non-fine arts majors, and 2) the active spirit surrounding social issues on and off campus. While I was co-director, we:

  • Hosted two art shows selling prints and original artworks to benefit local philanthropies
  • Started a creative resale supply initiative, providing gently used creative supplies to students
  • Collaborated with community organizers to raise awareness of social justice issues like homelessness, food insecurity, and reproductive rights
