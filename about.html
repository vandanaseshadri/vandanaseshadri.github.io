hello!

My name is Vandana (she/her), and I'm a creative person based in Dallas, TX. I have a B.S. in Chemical Engineering from the University of Texas at Austin.

I've been doing traditional analog artwork, like pencil drawing and oil/watercolor painting ever since I was little, and I continued my passion for artwork in college through Texas Visual Arts Collective, a student organization I co-founded and directed. After graduating, I've kept a regular creative practice; you can follow along here!

I'm currently working at IQVIA as a data analyst. I'm also an aspiring user researcher/product designer in the tech space. Outside of work and art, I like to swim, go on walks, play tennis, and indulge in pop culture.
