hello!

I’m Vandana, a visual artist, data analyst, and aspiring designer based in Dallas, Texas. I specialize in small-scale analog artwork, primarily working in watercolor, graphite, and pen/ink.

Using a design thinking framework, I think about how to create products and experiences to solve problems. I’ve developed creative solutions for university students and gathered user research in my current role as an analyst at IQVIA.

Outside of my career, I like to knit, write, and spend lots of time in the Texas sun.
